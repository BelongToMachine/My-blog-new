# Design Context

### Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and fellow developers evaluating Jie's frontend engineering skills and design sensibility. They browse this portfolio to quickly assess technical depth, attention to detail, and creative problem-solving.

### Brand Personality
Retro-pixel technical portfolio. Playful precision. Bilingual (Chinese-first, English-secondary). Confident without being arrogant.

### Aesthetic Direction
Retro-futuristic with pixel-art accents. Technically precise but warmly human. The site supports two modes:
- **Pixel mode**: hard edges, pixel fonts, scanlines, grid backgrounds, zero border-radius
- **Normal mode**: clean sans-serif, subtle shadows, rounded corners
Both share the same cyan/teal primary color family and maintain recruiter-facing polish.

### Design Principles
1. Preserve the pixel visual language — it's the portfolio's signature.
2. Technical content must be readable and scannable.
3. Motion should be purposeful, not decorative.
4. Maintain bilingual support throughout.
5. Every page should feel like a deliberate design choice, not a template.
